About Me

Coding is tough, tough stuff. But I kinda like it!

It's hard to explain what sparked the discovery that I wanted to learn to web development and design; there are so many reasons that understanding what makes the web work made sense to me.

I'm deeply interested in how content on the internet can be elegant, accessible, and enriching to all users from all walks of like. I'm inspired by people with more artsy than scientific frames of mind who are nonetheless committed to learning and teaching the ways of the markup. I hope to inspire others with my passion, my playfulness, and my pursuit of learning more for the betterment of all.

When I'm not marking it up, I'm probably busy obsessing over cinema, blasting beats, playing games (especially anything 16-bit or board related), or attempting to go for a hike or cook a nice meal. I'm a pretty simple guy, just trying to take pleasure in laughing, learning, and loving whenever I can.
